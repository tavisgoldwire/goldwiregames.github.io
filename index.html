What is Trophic?

Asymmetric Biomes

Each ecosystem operates differently. Arctic controls through storage and disruption. Savanna builds tempo through tuck synergy. Amazon creates explosive abundance. Choose your strategy.

The Cascade

Activate a producer at the base of your pyramid and watch energy cascade upward through your food web. Stack consumers and predators to build chains that fire every turn.

Interaction & Adaptation

Animals migrate between pyramids, stun opponents' cards, steal energy, and disrupt rival food webs. Adapt your hand to gain energy. Build smarter, not just bigger.
