What is Trophic?

Asymmetric Biomes

Each ecosystem operates differently. Arctic controls through disruption. Savanna builds tempo through speed. Amazon creates explosive abundance. Choose your strategy.

Engine-Building Food Webs

Stack producers, consumers, and predators into trophic pyramids. Activate your foundation and watch energy cascade upward through your ecosystem.

Interaction & Adaptation

Animals migrate, tap opponents' cards, steal resources, and disrupt rival food webs. Adapt your hand to gain energy. Build smarter, not just bigger.

Goldwire Games

We design science-informed board games that make ecosystems playable.

Trophic translates real ecological principles—trophic levels, energy transfer, niche competition—into asymmetric gameplay. Our goal is to create games that are accessible to casual players while honoring the complexity of natural systems.

Every card is researched. Every mechanic reflects how ecosystems actually work. And every game respects your intelligence.
