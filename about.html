Hello! I'm Tavis. I am currently pursuing my Master's in Zoology at the University of Florida, where my research focuses on the complex, invisible world of microbiomes.

I started Goldwire Games with a simple hypothesis: Board games are the perfect medium to teach systems thinking. In nature, nothing exists in isolation. Trophic cascades‚Äîwhere a change in the top predator affects the soil chemistry at the bottom‚Äîare the ultimate puzzle.

With TROPHIC, I wanted to move beyond "cute animal games" and build something that simulates the ruthless, beautiful balance of real ecosystems.

What Drives Us

Scientific Accuracy

No made-up stats. Every species ability in Trophic is derived from its actual biological niche and behavioral ecology.

Conservation

We put our money where our mouth is. 10% of all profits go directly to preserving the habitats you play on the board.

Education

Designed to be accessible for families but deep enough for classrooms. We are building curriculum guides to accompany the game.
